Instructions

- Position your hands shoulder width apart - Put your legs out in front of you, feet placed hip width apart - Straighten your arms, and keep a little bend in your elbows in order to always keep tension on your triceps and off your elbow joints - Slowly bend at your elbows, and lower your upper body toward the floor until your arms are at about a 90-degree angle - Once you reach the bottom of the movement, slowly press off with your hands, and push yourself straight back up to the starting position.
